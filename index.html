Hi. I'm Yifan Sun .

I am a 2 year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Shengjin Wang. I received both my B.E and M.E from Department of Precision Instrument, Tsinghua University, respectively on 2005 and 2008. Later on, I worked as an engineer in Beijing Institute of Telecommunication. Presently, my works focus on computer vision, especially deep-learning methods and person re-identification.
